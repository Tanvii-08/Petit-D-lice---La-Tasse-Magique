About Us



Petit Délice - La Tasse Magique



Welcome to Petit Délice - La Tasse Magique, where the elegance of a French café meets the vibrant flavors of Indian cuisine.

Our café-library is more than just a place to dine—it's an experience that blends culinary delights with the joy of reading. When you walk through the entrance of this café, you will instantly be feeling blissful with a sense of warmth and love that makes one feel joyful, with cool, cozy and calm ambiance. Yes, the interiors of The library Cafe are exactly that nostalgic and warm.

We take pride in offering a unique fusion menu that celebrates the best of both worlds, set against the backdrop of a cozy, book-filled environment. Whether you're here to enjoy a croissant with your coffee, explore our diverse library collection, or simply unwind in our welcoming space, Petit Délice invites you to savor every moment.
